How Zoopit aces B2B logistics

Our SaaS solution is delivered via two innovative applications — our Dashboard app for management and customer service teams and our Courier app for drivers — supported by a host of other key features to deliver effortless integration, KPI insights and tracking, and rich functionality.

Combined, Zoopit creates a seamless end-to-end B2B logistics service:

Step one

Integrate existing systems with Zoopit

Our powerful APIs allow your legacy systems to effortlessly integrate with Zoopit, enabling all existing and future orders to be automatically transferred.

Step two

Auto-generate and send optimised routes to drivers

Zoopit allows you to set routing rules based on capacity, pick-up location, and delivery windows before it automatically creates and sends optimised routes at lightning speed to your drivers. This maximises the efficiency of your fleet and operations with zero input needed from you — even last moment re-routing is managed by Zoopit.

Step three

Notify customers with auto-updates

Zoopit enables you to set customer comms parameters and then automatically notifies customers of expected delivery times as well as providing live updates if there are unforeseen delays.

Step four

Track deliveries right to the door

With real-time tracking via Dashboard’s map, Zoopit lets you know exactly where a package is and when it’s been delivered so you have visibility of your deliveries at a macro and micro level.

Step five

Make deliveries more simple

Your drivers can scan packages and record proof of delivery in moments — with each stage fully GDPR-compliant — making life easier for both you and your customers.

Total visibility 24/7

Customers can monitor where their packages are in real time while your business can track every stage of the delivery process — from what’s left in your warehouse and what has been loaded into your vehicles to where packages are and whether customers have received them.
